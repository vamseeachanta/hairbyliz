About Us

Elizabeth’s Hair Salon consists of 2 licensed professionals with over 20 years of experience who really listen to you and your needs to create personalized styles. Elizabeth Lopez (owner) and Elizabeth Valadez are both bilingual hair stylists with highly developed skills for all types of hair, and therefore are able to provide well rounded quality services to a wide ranged client base. Their focus and priority is on the needs of the diverse clientele so in order to stay on the cutting edge with their professional skills and techniques, they consistently attend hair shows and seminars.

Elizabeth Lopez

Elizabeth Lopez is the proud owner of Elizabeth’s Hair Salon. Becoming a successful licensed Hair Stylist and owning a hair salon is Elizabeth’s dream come true. Hair styling has been her passion since she was a little girl living in El Salvador, where she was born and raised. Elizabeth eventually made her way to Houston, Texas where she began to pursue her career. In 1990, Elizabeth attended Wilfred Beauty Academy (located in the Heights - Houston Texas). Once she graduated and earned her Cosmetology License, she started her career.

1991 - 1993: Hair Stylist at TGF Haircutters
1993 – 1998: Hair Stylist and Shift Manager at Supercuts
1998 – 2008: Independent Contractor at Simply Tina’s

In 2008, Elizabeth took over Simply Tina’s. She remodeled and renamed the building. 2008 – Present: Owner of Elizabeth’s Hair Salon

Aside from a busy work schedule, Elizabeth carries on a very busy life, but still makes time to lend a helping hand. Elizabeth volunteers for a non-profit organization. She is involved in missionary activities, which include traveling to places such as El Salvador and Mexico to help people in need. If she is able to help in any way, Elizabeth always will.

Not only does she love doing hair, but Elizabeth loves the relationship she builds with her clients. She has a very big heart and she will become more than your average well-rounded hair stylist; you will find a true friend in Elizabeth!
